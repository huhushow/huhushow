What is paging?

A technique that divides a large amount of data into a certain number of pieces. 많은 수의 자료를 일정한 숫자 만큼 나누어 보여주는 기술

Why using paging?

Both human and machine would hard to handle a large amount of data. 사람과 기계 모두 한번에 많은 수의 데이터를 처리하기엔 힘들기 때문

How paging works?

  1. Front-end gives to back-end their data range
  2. Back-end loads data by given range from front-end
  3. Also back-end gives summary of total data to front-end. that data used by paging function.
